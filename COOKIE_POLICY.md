# Cookie Policy

**Last Updated: November 12, 2025**

## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences and understanding how you use our platform.

## Types of Cookies We Use

### 1. Necessary Cookies (Always Active)

These cookies are essential for the website to function properly. They cannot be disabled.

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration |
|-------------|---------|----------|
| `session_id` | Maintains your login session | Session |
| `csrf_token` | Prevents cross-site request forgery | Session |
| `cookieConsent` | Remembers your cookie preferences | 1 year |

### 2. Functional Cookies (Optional)

These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization.

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration |
|-------------|---------|----------|
| `user_preferences` | Stores your UI preferences | 1 year |
| `language` | Remembers your language choice | 1 year |
| `theme` | Stores your theme preference (dark/light) | 1 year |

### 3. Analytics Cookies (Optional)

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website.

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration | Provider |
|-------------|---------|----------|----------|
| `_ga` | Google Analytics visitor ID | 2 years | Google |
| `_gid` | Google Analytics session ID | 24 hours | Google |
| `_gat` | Google Analytics throttling | 1 minute | Google |

**Data Collected**: Page views, session duration, bounce rate, geographic location (country/city), device type, browser type.

**Purpose**: Improve user experience, identify popular features, detect technical issues.

### 4. Marketing Cookies (Optional)

These cookies are used to deliver personalized content and advertisements.

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration | Provider |
|-------------|---------|----------|----------|
| `_fbp` | Facebook Pixel | 3 months | Facebook |
| `IDE` | Google Ads | 13 months | Google |
| `test_cookie` | Ad personalization | 15 minutes | Google |

**Purpose**: Show relevant ads, measure ad campaign effectiveness, remarketing.

## How to Manage Cookies

### Option 1: Cookie Banner

When you first visit our website, you'll see a cookie banner where you can:
- Accept all cookies
- Accept only necessary cookies
- Customize your preferences

### Option 2: Browser Settings

You can also control cookies through your browser settings:

**Chrome**
1. Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
2. Choose your preferred option

**Firefox**
1. Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
2. Choose your preferred option

**Safari**
1. Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
2. Remove or block cookies

**Edge**
1. Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies and site data
2. Choose your preferred option

### Option 3: Opt-Out Tools

- Google Analytics: [https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout)
- Facebook: [https://www.facebook.com/settings?tab=ads](https://www.facebook.com/settings?tab=ads)
- Your Online Choices: [https://www.youronlinechoices.com/](https://www.youronlinechoices.com/)

## Local Storage and Session Storage

In addition to cookies, we use browser storage technologies:

### Local Storage
- User preferences

### Session Storage
- Temporary form data
- Navigation state
- Unsaved work recovery

## Impact of Disabling Cookies

If you disable cookies, some features may not work properly:

| Cookie Type | Impact of Disabling |
|-------------|-------------------|
| Necessary | Website will not function properly |
| Functional | Loss of personalization and preferences |
| Analytics | No impact on functionality, but we can't improve the service based on usage data |
| Marketing | No personalized ads (may see generic ads instead) |
